The History Of The Computer

1970s

  • [1971] Intel 4004 (First Microprocessor).
  • [1972] The C programming language is released.
  • [1976] Gary Kildall develops the first commercially successful operating system for microcomputers, CP/M.
  • [1977] Star Wars combined old-fashioned sci-fi storytelling with cutting-edge special effects provided by Industrial Light & Magic it was one of the first uses of wire-frame animation in a major motion picture.
  • [1978] First computers installed in the White House.

1980s

  • Intel 8088; Motorola 68000; Zilog Z8000 (1979)

1990s

  • DEC VAX DC520 Rigel; Intel 80486, i860 (1989)

2000s

  • AMD Athlon; IBM RS64-III; Intel Pentium III; Motorola PowerPC 7400 (1999)

2010s

  • AMD Opteron Istanbul, Phenom II (2009)
