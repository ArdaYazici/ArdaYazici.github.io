The History Of The Computer

1970s

  • Intel 4004 (First Microprocessor). [1971]
  • The C programming language is released. [1972]
  • Gary Kildall develops the first commercially successful operating system for microcomputers, CP/M. [1976]
  • Set in a Galaxy far, far away, Star Wars combined old-fashioned science fiction storytelling with cutting-edge special effects provided by Industrial Light & Magic. One effect, the Death Star briefing, featured a wire-frame version of the space station, one of the first uses of wire-frame animation in a major motion picture. [1977]
  • First computers installed in the White House. [1978]

1980s

  • Intel 8088; Motorola 68000; Zilog Z8000 (1979)

1990s

  • DEC VAX DC520 Rigel; Intel 80486, i860 (1989)

2000s

  • AMD Athlon; IBM RS64-III; Intel Pentium III; Motorola PowerPC 7400 (1999)

2010s

  • AMD Opteron Istanbul, Phenom II (2009)
