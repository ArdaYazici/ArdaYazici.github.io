NBA player reportedly suspended for throwing soup at assistant coach

Cleveland Cavaliers guard J.R. Smith was suspended one game by the team for throwing a bowl of soup at assistant coach Damon Jones, two people with knowledge of the situation told USA TODAY Sports on Friday.

Tiger Woods commits to next two tournaments

In another positive sign in his latest comeback, Tiger Woods on Friday committed to play next week’s Valspar Championship and the following week’s Arnold Palmer Invitational, which he has won eight times.
