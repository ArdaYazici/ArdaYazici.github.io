Asus ROG Zephyrus GX501

Product Specifications

Price: CAD $3194.99
CPU: Intel Core i7-7700HQ (Quad Core 3.8GHz)
Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 1080 + Intel HD Graphics 630
RAM: 16GB DDR4
Screen: 15.6-inch, Full HD (120Hz with Nvidia G-Sync)
Storage: 512GB SSD

Positives

  • Extremely Powerful
  • Innovative, Quiet Cooling

Negatives

  • Terribly Low Battery Life
  • No SD Slot

Target Market

The Asus ROG Zephyrus GX501 is targeted toward the wealthy people of the world who enjoy gaming and like to play their video games with high end performances.
